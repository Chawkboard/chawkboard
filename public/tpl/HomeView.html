This is a Chawk server. Chawk is an engine that lets you store little pieces of data and sort, display and aggregate them together to make more sense out of them.

Everyone wants to save on fuel costs and reduce impact on the environment. Chawk can let you store data directly from your car - and compare it to weather conditions, traffic, fuel prices and any other data - years later.
